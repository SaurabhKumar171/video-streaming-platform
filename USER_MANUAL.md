# 🎬 V-Stream Multi-Tenant Portal -- User Manual

Welcome to the **V-Stream Multi-Tenant Portal**. This guide walks you
through everything from onboarding your organization to managing and
streaming videos with AI-powered insights.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🛡️ 1. Getting Started: The Registry

The platform follows a **Multi-Tenant Architecture**, ensuring complete
data isolation between organizations.

### 🆕 Creating a New Organization

1.  Navigate to the **Register** page.
2.  Select **"New Workspace"**.
3.  Enter your **Organization Name** (e.g., *Global Tech*).
4.  Set your **Admin credentials**.

> ⚠️ **Important:** After registration, go to your **Dashboard** to find
> your **Unique Tenant ID**.\
> You will need this to invite team members.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 👥 Joining an Existing Team

1.  Obtain the **Tenant ID** from your administrator.
2.  Select **"Join Existing"** on the Register page.
3.  Paste the ID.
4.  Choose your role:
    -   👁️ Viewer
    -   ✏️ Editor

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📤 2. The Upload Engine

The platform uses **Cloud-Native Ingestion** for fast and reliable
uploads.

### Steps:

1.  Click on **"Upload Engine"** from the sidebar.
2.  Drag & drop your video file.

### Supported Formats:

-   MP4
-   MOV
-   AVI

### Add Metadata:

-   Title
-   Category:
    -   Tutorial
    -   Meeting
    -   Marketing

3.  Click **"Launch Ingestion"**

> ⚠️ **Note:** Do NOT refresh the page until upload reaches **100%**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🤖 3. AI Analysis & Safety

Once uploaded, the **AI Sentinel** automatically audits your video.

### 🔍 Features:

-   **Frame Scanning:** Detects restricted or sensitive content
-   **Sensitivity Score:** Generated from **0% -- 100%**
-   **Auto-Flagging:**
    -   75% → 🚨 Flagged for admin review

### 📌 Status Indicators:

-   ⏳ **Processing** → AI is analyzing
-   ✅ **Completed** → Ready for streaming

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📺 4. Cinematic Playback & Quality

Enjoy a **Netflix-style adaptive streaming experience**.

### 🎞️ Features:

-   **Dynamic Scaling:**
    -   Switch between:
        -   Original
        -   720p
        -   480p
-   **Global Delivery:**
    -   Powered by CDN for **zero buffering**
-   **Security Metadata Panel:** Displays:
    -   Payload (file size)
    -   Codec
    -   Security Clearance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📊 5. Administrative Controls

Admins have complete control over platform operations.

### 📈 Dashboard Stats:

-   Cloud Payload (Total storage)
-   Active Assets

### 🧾 Audit Log:

-   Track:
    -   Upload events
    -   Flagging
    -   Transcoding

### ❌ Decommissioning:

-   Click **Delete** icon to remove video
-   Permanently deletes from:
    -   Database
    -   CDN Storage

## 🧠 Best Practices

-   Always verify **Tenant ID** before sharing
-   Monitor flagged content regularly
-   Avoid interrupting uploads
-   Optimize video size for faster ingestion
